Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ZaderFood ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal data is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and smart meal planning services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, and login credentials when you register.
  • Health & Dietary Data: Information you provide for meal planning, such as age, gender, weight, height, activity level, dietary preferences, and food allergies.
  • User Content: Images of food uploaded for AI analysis, recipes you create, and meal logs.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with the site, including traffic data and logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for specific purposes:

  • To generate personalized meal plans and calculate nutritional needs (TDEE/BMR).
  • To provide AI-based food recognition services.
  • To improve our algorithms and user experience.
  • To send you shopping lists, updates, or essential account notifications.

4. AI Processing

Our application uses AI models (such as Ollama) to process text and images. When you use features like "AI Food Scanner" or "Generate Meal Plan," relevant data (e.g., food images, diet constraints) is processed to provide the service. We do not use your personal health data for purposes other than providing the requested functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your passwords are hashed, and sensitive connections are secured via SSL/TLS.
